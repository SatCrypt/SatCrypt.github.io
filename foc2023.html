Prerequisites:

I assume the students to have basic familiarity with probability theory, elementary number theory, elementary discrete mathematics, linear algebra and algorithms. It is advisable to take this course only if you have the necessary background and interest in the field of cryptography. Prior exposure to cryptography is helpful but not necessary.
